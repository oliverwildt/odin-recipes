Pizza

This is the website you want to come to when you want to make some delicous pizza.

These pizzas take the best from italian and american style pizzas to guarantee maximum pleasure!

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomatos
  • Onions
  • Chicken
  • Cheese
  • Barbeque Sauce

Steps

  1. Whip up the pizza dough
  2. Put the ingredients on top!
  3. Put it in the oven!
  4. Wait for 10 minutes!
  5. Enjoy!
