Hamburgers!

This site will provide a recipe to create delicous hamburgers!

Please give them a try!

Ingredients

  • Burger Patties
  • Burger Buns
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatos
  • Onions
  • Cheese
  • Ketchup

Steps

  1. Put burger patties on the grill!
  2. Prepare the tomatos and onions
  3. Slice up the cheese
  4. Dish out the ketchup
  5. Put all the ingredients between the burger buns
